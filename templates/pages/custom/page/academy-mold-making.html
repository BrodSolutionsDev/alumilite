LESSON PLAN

7 CHAPTERS • 66 VIDEOS • 3HR 30MIN

  • CHAPTER 1: PLANNING & PREPPING

    Join Jordan in his discussion about the planning and prepping stages of crafting basic molds. He’ll teach you how to conceptualize molds for whatever master you choose and how to begin turning that concept into a reality. Also gain insight into what kinds of silicones to use and what qualities of theirs you should pay attention to.

  • CHAPTER 2: BUILDING

    Jordan walks you through the tools you’ll need to build your own mold box as well as the different types of mold boxes you can create. You’ll also learn the best ways for adhering your master in your mold box and positioning your mold box for pouring.

  • CHAPTER 3: MIXING & POURING

    Discover how to best tackle measuring, mixing, and pouring silicone for a custom mold. Jordan also breaks down the processes of degassing and pressure casting silicone.

  • CHAPTER 4: CURING

    Did you know that there are numerous types of curing processes? Let’s take a look at what those are, the ways you can test a silicone’s cure, and the difference between a demolding cure and a full cure.

  • CHAPTER 5: DEMOLDING

    Once your mold has cured, the next step is demolding. What does demolding entail? Jordan gives us insight into removing our mold boxes, trimming excess silicone, and the variety of release helpers you can use while demolding your mold.

  • CHAPTER 6: MOLD SUPPORT & POSITIONING

    What are support cradles and when should you implement them? What can you do to ensure the best casting results from your mold? Jordan breaks it down (figuratively) so that you can set yourself up for success (literally).

  • CHAPTER 7: MOLD CARE & MAINTENANCE

    How do you care for your mold once it’s complete? In order to ensure the longevity of your custom mold, proper storage and maintenance are essential. Learn more about what that looks like.

WHO IT’S FOR: This course is designed for those with a new or developing interest in basic mold making. The lessons not only discuss the process, but necessary safety precautions as well as a breakdown of some of the technical language used in the industry.
