LESSON PLAN

5 CHAPTERS • 12 VIDEOS • 45MIN

  • CHAPTER 1: PLANNING & PREPPING

    Follow along as Jordan performs a master scan on a basic object to prep for making a mold, talks mold design with specific examples, and breaks down calculating the volume of silicone needed.

  • CHAPTER 2: SILICONE

    There are many factors to consider when choosing a silicone for your mold. Jordan introduces those considerations and walks you through each of them in this silicone topic deep dive.

  • CHAPTER 3: MOLD BOX

    Now that we’ve decided on material, let’s take a look at how we’re going to build our mold box. Also featured in this lesson is prepping your master to be molded and some quick solutions if things don’t go the way you planned.

  • CHAPTER 4: CURING

    To mix and pour silicone properly, we need to review our basic measuring info and any product specifications, implement the proper mixing practices, and execute the best pouring procedures.

  • CHAPTER 5: DEMOLDING

    Finally, Jordan demolds our project. See how to put into practice those demolding techniques discussed in the basics course and the process of cleaning up any flash that may have resulted from the curing process.

WHO IT’S FOR: This course is designed for those with a new or developing interest in basic mold making. The lessons not only discuss the process, but necessary safety precautions as well as a breakdown of some of the technical language used in the industry.
